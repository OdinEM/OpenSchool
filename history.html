History Curriculum

Ancient Civilizations

Explore the beginnings of human society, including Mesopotamia, Ancient Egypt, the Indus Valley, and China.

Classical Antiquity

Dive into the rich histories of Greece and Rome, exploring their cultures, politics, and philosophies.

Medieval Europe

Understand the Middle Ages in Europe, covering feudalism, the Church, and the rise of kingdoms.

Renaissance and Reformation

Discover the cultural rebirth of Europe and the religious transformations that shaped the modern world.

Modern History

Learn about the key events from the 18th century to the present, including revolutions, wars, and social changes.

Contemporary World

Analyze the recent history, focusing on globalization, technology, and contemporary issues.
